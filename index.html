Lesbians Aloud

Welcome to the Lesbians Aloud website!

Our website is for lesbians of all ages who are looking for a community to belong to where we can enjoy the arts together. It is our aim to bring lesbians together through theatre, poetry, singing and music.

In the absence of physical lesbian spaces, the main source of networking seems to be a myriad of Facebook groups which come and go. During the covid lockdown, one of our members started holding fortnightly zoom meetings known as Lesbians Under Lockdown (LUL). The majority of attendees were older Lesbians, many living alone and not in support bubbles. The negative impacts of isolation were prevalent within this group.

Through the Arts we will recreate our community!

Creative Genres

Theatre

Exploring our lesbian lives through the mediums of theatre and dance. Telling our stories to gather a greater understanding of the trials and tribulations of our personal lived experiences as lesbians.

Poetry

Never has a lesbians life been expressed more beautifully than it is when using our spoken and written word, poetry. Romantic, emotional, and often hard hitting, our lesbian lives laid bare.

Singing

When we sing, we express our lesbian lived experience in a way that quite literally resonates through the room and our very beings. There is nothing more powerful and liberating!

Music

Whether you are banging on a samba drum, strumming on your guitar, or revealing the sensitive emotion of a violin, making music is good for the soul. Let your lesbian soul be revatilised.

Events

What's on?

Why not bring yourself along to one of our Lesbians Aloud events and find sisterhood and community, whilst enjoying one of our performances or workshops?

Our events are the results of our forming a lesbian community of creatives and we create our productions based on the experiences that we share as lesbians within our community. Everything about what we do is about creating and redefining the communities that lesbians need in the world today.
